About the game

Football Player is a text-based football career simulation game where you control the life and decisions of a professional football player. In this game, you don’t control matches directly. Instead, you make strategic choices that influence your career, such as training routines, lifestyle decisions, transfers, relationships with teammates and coaches, and how you react to important events throughout the season. Each decision affects your player’s attributes, energy, finances, and reputation. Your choices can lead to success, stagnation, or unexpected consequences, making every playthrough unique. The game focuses on realism through decision-making rather than graphics, allowing players to experience the challenges of a football career in a simple and accessible way. Progress through seasons, improve your skills, manage your resources, and see how your career unfolds based on your actions. Football Player is designed for football fans who enjoy strategy, storytelling, and management-style gameplay.
